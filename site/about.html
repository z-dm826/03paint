Our Story

Creating a quiet refuge from the noise of daily life, one brushstroke at a time.

Why we started

Atelier Lumière was founded on the belief that the art you live with should slow you down. In a world of constant motion, we wanted to create pieces that invite stillness — works that hold a wall with calm, confidence, and texture.

We began by studying the originals: the way light falls across impasto, the subtle shifts between warm and cool, the quiet rhythm of abstract composition. Then we rebuilt that experience as a faithful reproduction, preserving depth and detail so collectors can live with a gallery presence every day.

Our Process

From digital proof to finished canvas.

1. Capture the Original

We begin with ultra-high-resolution photography and colour calibration, recording every ridge, gradient, and subtle tone.

2. Build the Texture

Our artisans apply layers of archival gesso and pigment, rebuilding the relief by hand so the surface catches light like the original.

3. Inspect & Finish

Each piece is reviewed for colour accuracy and depth, then sealed with a protective, UV-stable varnish before framing or stretching.

4. Ship with Care

We package each work in reinforced crates or tubes, then ship via insured courier to your door, ready to hang.
