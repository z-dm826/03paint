Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before ordering your artwork.

Each piece is made to order. Production takes 5–7 business days, and shipping is typically 3–7 business days depending on your location. You will receive tracking information as soon as your artwork is on its way.
We offer three finishes: Rolled Canvas (frameless, ships in a tube), Stretched & Ready-to-Hang (mounted on a solid wood frame with hooks), and Framed in Solid Wood (a clean floating frame in natural or black wood).
For a single statement above a sofa or bed, Large or X-Large usually works best. For narrow hallways or intimate corners, Small or Medium is ideal. If you are unsure, email us a photo of your wall and we will recommend the right scale.
Yes, we ship worldwide via insured courier. Free shipping is available on orders over $400. For remote destinations, additional transit time may apply.
We offer a 30-day no-risk money-back guarantee. If your artwork does not feel right in your space, contact us and we will arrange a return or exchange. Custom commissions are non-refundable, but we will share proofs before final production.
